Houston Dynamo FC use TAM to Sign Attacker Amine Bassi

Houston Dynamo FC used Target Allocation Money to sign attacker Amine Bassi through the 2024 season with a club option for 2025 and 2026. Bassi began his professional career with AS Nancy-Lorraine in his native France, where he scored 26 goals for the club and added 17 assists in 119 appearances in all competitions. He joins the Dynamo on a full transfer from FC Metz in France’s Ligue 2, pending the receipt of his P-1 Visa and International Transfer Certificate (ITC).

Houston Dash Forward Ryan Gareis Signs Contract Extension

Houston Dash forward Ryan Gareis signed a contract extension through the 2025 season. Gareis made an immediate impact during her rookie season with 6 starts and 15 appearances during Houston’s historic 2022 season.

Leagues Cup 2023 Group Stage


Major League Soccer and LIGA MX announced the groups for the 2023 Leagues Cup. Houston Dynamo FC will compete against Orlando City SC and Santos Laguna from Mexico in one of the four groups in the South Region.

Houston Dynamo Football Club Signs Agreement with Arca Continental Coca-Cola Southwest Beverages


Houston Dynamo Football Club announced a sponsorship agreement with Arca Continental Coca-Cola Southwest Beverages (CCSWB) that establishes Coca-Cola as the exclusive soft drink and beverage partner of the Club for the next five years. CCSWB will revolutionize beverage offerings at the Stadium for fans, as well as provide Hustle Town Supporters with complimentary water throughout the season.

Houston Dynamo FC Hire SRC FTBL to Build Analytics Department


Houston Dynamo FC announced a multi-year agreement with Houston-based SRC FTBL (Source Football) to build an analytics department for the club with a focus on player identification and first team performance. SRC FTBL will guide the development of the club’s analytics department while supporting the first team and scouting department.

Houston Dynamo FC and Texas Bay Credit Union Announce New Partnership


Houston Dynamo FC and Texas Bay Credit Union have partnered to make banking easy through a new partnership agreement. The agreement makes Texas Bay Credit Union an official credit union of Houston Dynamo FC.
